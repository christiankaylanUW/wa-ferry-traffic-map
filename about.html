About This Webmap

Overview:

Our map is an interactive web GIS that visualizes the ferry port traffic in the Port of Seattle across the central Puget Sound region. It combines a custom-designed Mapbox basemap with ferry route data and time-based vessel information obtained from the Washington State Department of Transportation (WSDOT). Riders can utilize this map to better understand ferry traffic and scheduling, implementing this app into their daily and regional commute.

Features:

  • Simulated Ferry View: Simulated overview of ferry movement across the Puget Sound to detail ship movement.

  • Live Ferry View: Updated real-time locations of ships, utilizing WSDOT API.
    1. Ferry Information, Schedule, and Port locations.

Data Sources:

  • WSDOT Ferry Routes Geojson (Source)
  • Ferry Terminals + Routes in Seattle (Source)
