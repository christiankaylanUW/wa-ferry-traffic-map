Welcome to the WA State Ferry Map!



This map provides real-time locations of Washington State Ferries, along with terminal schedule information. To get started, select a ferry terminal from the dropdown menu or click a terminal on the map. You will then see that terminals schedule for the day, as well as the real-time capacity of each departing ferry. You can also switch between simulated and real-time ferry positions using the toggle in the bottom-right corner. Click any ferry to view its current status, including its origin, destination, estimated arrival time, and current speed.
